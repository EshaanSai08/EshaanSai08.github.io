Itinerary

*Expeditions are offered ONLY as package deals. Customers must attend all destinations listed on the itinerary below.*

The Moon: Earth's Luminous Companion

Your voyage begins with a visit to the Moon, our closest celestial neighbor. Walk in the footsteps of astronauts as you explore lunar landscapes, marvel at the stunning Earthrise, and discover the history and allure of our lunar companion.

Venus: The Enigmatic Twin

Next, we'll transport you to Venus, Earth's mysterious sister planet. Experience the unique challenges of its scorching surface and explore the secrets hidden beneath its thick, acidic clouds. Witness unforgettable Venusian sunsets that paint the skies with fiery hues.

Mars: The Red Frontier

Continue your journey to Mars, the iconic "Red Planet." Traverse its rugged terrain, explore ancient river valleys, and follow in the footsteps of rovers that have unveiled its mysteries. Witness the beauty of Martian sunsets and gaze at the starry nights over the rusty sands.

The Kuiper Belt: Beyond Neptune's Realm

Your adventure culminates with a visit to the Kuiper Belt, a region beyond Neptune teeming with frozen worlds and cosmic relics. Explore the frontier where comets and dwarf planets reside, unlocking the secrets of our solar system's history.
